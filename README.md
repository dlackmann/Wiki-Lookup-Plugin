# Wiki Lookup

Right-click any item in your inventory and choose **Wiki Lookup** to pull up its OSRS Wiki page directly in the sidebar.

The panel shows the item's members status, Grand Exchange price, High Alch value, and equipment stats — including a comparison against whatever you currently have equipped in that slot. There's also a button to open the full article in your browser.

Works on inventory items and optionally on ground items (toggle in settings).
